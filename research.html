Why this field matters

What does the evidence say?

The need is real and measured. Canada's first national study of music professionals (n = 1,216) puts the gap between this workforce and the general population in plain numbers.A

86% of music professionals personally experienced mental-health challengesvs. ~12% of the general workforce
43% have had lifetime suicidal thoughtsvs. ~12% in the general population
Prevalence findings across six studies · each row traced to its paperB
