01

Knowledge & Findings

The cycle produced the largest body of work in the project's history — and it points at everywhere except home.

The country-parity build landed at scale

Between June 27 and June 29 the project shipped 17 organization studies and 6 policy studies — Japan, Germany, Thailand, South Korea, the UK, the US, Canada, Argentina, the EU — through parallel scheduled autoruns, each fact-checked and deployed.2 The wiki grew from 22 articles to 56 (8 concepts, 30 orgs, 17 papers): +34 in a fortnight.3 The Dia-grade reading-environment scale was rolled site-wide across every deploy page on June 29.5 By raw output this is, by a wide margin, the biggest cycle the project has ever run.

This was commissioned work, not drift. 陳棉 retired the six-region policy plan on June 28 in favour of country-parity — giving each studied jurisdiction both an org study and a policy study — and scheduled the autorun batches to execute it.8 The comparative corpus that resulted is genuine, defensible research IP: the policy studies converged on a real analytic — a rights floor (labour and occupational-health protections a state guarantees) versus a services room (the discretionary support an NPO can build on top).7 That distinction is worth having, and it did not exist a month ago.

Sort the output by track, and the finding appears

Every jurisdiction studied this cycle is abroad. Taiwan — the country the project exists to serve — received neither an org study nor a policy study, and remains the one blank tile on an otherwise near-complete map.6 The NPO/Taiwan primary track produced nothing again: raw/notes/ is empty, no name, no date, no conversation on record. The contact count stands at zero for the fourth consecutive insight.6

Intake didn't outrun digestion — it lapped it

The June 5 and June 12 health checks flagged three items: Cerdá-Díaz 2026 orphaned (Markdown only, no wiki article, deploy page, or card), and two April-supplied raw files — Bergson et al. 2023 and the JWF Annual Report — uncompiled since the April 11 check. All three are still open.4 The project added 34 new wiki articles while the same two files sat uncompiled for roughly thirteen weeks, and the named §10 reference implementation stayed orphaned ~3.5 weeks past its flag. New reading was not just added ahead of the queue; the queue was lapped.

The comparative deck is now the most complete a Taiwan music-MH founder could hold. Its very completeness is the tell: every domain has deepened except the one that needs a person on the other end of a message rather than a page to fetch.

What is unchanged — and still the whole case

The core advocacy material has not moved because it did not need to: Musgrave & Lamis 2025 (US male musicians at 138.7 per 100,000), the MusiCares Wellness in Music Survey as the operational template, Soundcheck / Harrison 2026 (Canada, n=1,216) as the methodology for a Taiwan-equivalent study.9 The first-mover thesis holds. It has now held, unused, for five insights.

Trajectory

June 18 named the avoidance "productivity." This cycle scaled the productivity to record volume — and the one number that matters is unchanged.

The pattern didn't persist; it scaled

June 18 said the avoidance had changed costume from silence to productivity, and that this was the more sophisticated form of the same pattern.1 July does not soften that reading — it enlarges it. The single biggest production burst in the project's history landed in one weekend, and the NPO-contact count moved from zero to zero.6 Four consecutive insights, one unbroken zero.

But the honest correction: this was chosen, not evaded

Unlike the infrastructure churn of June, the country-parity build was an explicit strategic decision 陳棉 made and scheduled.8 So the fairer frame is not "avoidance wearing productivity" but a genuine doubling-down on the academic/comparative identity. The two tracks have not merely diverged, as June 18 concluded — the comparative-research track has, in practice, won. The project is now, in fact if not in name, a comparative-policy and landscape-research project that keeps an NPO track branded and dormant.

The map is near-saturation — which retires the last unspoken excuse

With roughly ten jurisdictions and twenty organizations now mapped, the comparative landscape is close to complete. There is little world left to map. The rationale that has always been implied but never stated — I need more landscape before I can act — is now empirically exhausted: every additional study returns less, and the one tile that would return the most (Taiwan) is precisely the one the mapping never reaches, because it is the one that requires a phone call instead of a web search.6

03

Direction Reflections

June 18 offered a binary and a June 24 gate. The project answered with a third option June 18 never listed: keep mapping.

The revealed preference is now unmistakable

June 18 asked for one of two things before June 24: one irreversible Taiwan act, or an honest rename to academic-first (Option B).1 Neither happened. Instead the project executed the largest map-expansion in its history. That is a decision — the loudest one yet — and it is keep mapping the world. The drift is no longer quiet; it is a stated strategy with a scheduled backlog.

The blank tile is the whole tell

The atlas is the achievement and the diagnosis at once. Nine countries carry a full policy study; Taiwan carries none.6 Taiwan is not unmapped because its data is scarce — it is unmapped because mapping it requires the single act the project reliably defers: a primary contact (a real person, a message, a reply) rather than a secondary source (a page to fetch, a filing to read). The project has built a world-class ability to study systems from the outside and has not once stepped inside the one it means to change.

What it needs is unchanged and now absurdly cheap by comparison

The effort spent on 23 country studies would have covered a single Taiwan conversation a hundred times over. The research deck is not just loaded; it is now the most comprehensive comparative deck a Taiwan music-MH founder could hold.9 The missing step has never been more evidence. It is putting the evidence already in hand in front of one real person at home.

The binary, re-anchored to an event that fires on its own

Willpower dates have failed three times (May 31, June 14, June 24). The next thing that fires without anyone's willpower is the wiki health check on July 5, and after it the next scheduled study batch.8 Before the next country study is authored, one of two things should be true.

A
Fill the Taiwan tile
One message to one real person in Taiwan's music scene, and one line filed in raw/notes/, regardless of reply. The smallest unit of the work the project exists to do, still missing.
B
Rename the atlas
If not, adopt Option B in writing: comparative-research-first, NPO track and its branded scheduled tasks explicitly paused — so the schedule stops manufacturing a two-track story one track never touches.

Either is clean. The only unclean outcome is the current one: a complete map of everywhere but home, and a monthly synthesis left to absorb the difference.

Workflow & Collaboration

Five workflow personas, grounded in this cycle's record — two proposals re-issued with teeth, one moved-forward kill criterion, two non-proposals.

Workflow Optimizer
Organization
Lens: session cadence, allocation, throughput, context-switching

Throughput is not the constraint and this cycle removed the last doubt: ~23 country studies authored, fact-checked, and deployed across roughly three days via parallel autoruns.2 The machine is extraordinary. But re-proposing June 18's "infrastructure freeze" is moot — it was proposed and then inverted at record scale: ルール/, design-system.html, and the site-wide reading-environment all churned.5 A freeze the project overrides within a fortnight is not a constraint; it is a note.

Proposal 1 of 2 — Single-tile gate on the study pipeline

No new country study (org or policy) is authored or scheduled until the Taiwan tile has one primary entry in raw/notes/. Mechanical and auditable: the study autoruns stay disabled, and the next insight checks raw/notes/ for one dated Taiwan line before any new batch is queued. Redirect the proven throughput at the one tile it keeps skipping.

Research Methodologist
Academic
Lens: pipeline efficiency, health-check → action gap, intake discipline

June 18's "drain before intake" was not just skipped — it was inverted. +34 wiki articles were added while Cerdá-Díaz stayed orphaned and Bergson 2023 + the JWF report stayed uncompiled ~13 weeks.4 The diagnostic system works; the response system has now ignored the same three findings across four health checks. The July 5 wiki check (fires tomorrow) will otherwise log this debt a fourth time.8

Proposal 2 of 2 — Drain before intake, now mandatory and prior to any study

The next research/study session of any kind reads and authors nothing new until: the Cerdá-Díaz triple is finished (wiki + deploy + card, born compliant) and Bergson 2023 + JWF are compiled into wiki/. Rule: the backlog empties before the corpus grows again.

Strategic Mentor
Life Direction
Lens: energy allocation, phase transition (building → using), honest labeling

June said capability was no longer an excuse — you shipped a citation system in a week. July says more: you mapped ten countries in a weekend.2 The capacity is not merely present; it is enormous, and it is aimed with precision at every country except the one you live in. Sit with the specific shape of the avoidance: not idleness, not incompetence, but a world-class study of everyone else's house while your own stays unvisited — because visiting it means a message that can go unanswered, and the brain reliably prefers the study that cannot reject you.

The honest label is no longer "two-track project, one track stalled." It is a comparative-research project keeping a dormant NPO track alive to preserve a founding story. There is real work here and it is good — but it is not the work the NPO track was for, and calling it that is the cost that compounds.

The proposal is the same binary as §3, on you personally

Fill the Taiwan tile with one primary contact before the next study batch, or rename the atlas in writing and stop scheduling the guilt. Drift is the only disrespectful option left.
